Privacy Policy - Block Game

Last updated: January 29, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Block Game ("the Game", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you play our game. This policy is specific to Block Game and supplements the general PassiveProject Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

When you play Block Game, we may collect:

  • Game Progress: Your game scores, levels completed, and achievements
  • Usage Data: Time spent playing, game sessions, and gameplay statistics
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and screen resolution
  • Local Storage: Game saves and preferences stored on your device

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Save your game progress and settings
  • Provide personalized gaming experience
  • Track achievements and leaderboards
  • Improve game performance and fix bugs
  • Develop new features and game modes

Data Storage

Block Game stores most data locally on your device using browser local storage. This means your game progress is saved on your device and is not automatically synced to our servers unless you explicitly enable cloud sync features.

Third-Party Services

Block Game may use third-party analytics services to understand how players interact with the game. These services may collect information sent by your device. We only work with services that respect user privacy.

Children's Privacy

Block Game is designed to be family-friendly and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, since most data is stored locally on your device, we recommend using device security features like passwords and encryption.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your local game data at any time through your browser settings
  • Opt out of analytics tracking
  • Request deletion of any server-stored data
  • Access information about what data we collect

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository or refer to the general PassiveProject Privacy Policy.
